What It Is

LiVE is a free, privacy-first tool that transforms your LinkedIn data export into actionable insights about your professional network.

LinkedIn lets you download your data, but gives you raw CSV files that are nearly impossible to make sense of. LiVE takes those files and instantly shows you:

Who you actually talk to — and who you've never messaged despite being "connected" for years.

Where your network is concentrated — which companies, industries, and seniority levels dominate your connections.

Who advocates for you — the people who've endorsed your skills and written recommendations.

What LinkedIn thinks about you — the (often hilariously wrong) inferences LinkedIn makes for ad targeting.

Where the opportunities are — dormant relationships worth reviving and strategic contacts to prioritize.

Think of it as a dashboard for your professional network that LinkedIn should have built but didn't.

How It Works

  • •
    100% Browser-Based Processing
    All analysis runs locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your computer—no server uploads, no cloud processing, no data collection.
  • •
    ZIP Extraction via JSZip
    The JSZip library extracts CSV files directly from your LinkedIn export ZIP without needing to unzip it first.
  • •
    CSV Parsing & Data Enrichment
    Custom CSV parser handles LinkedIn's export format. Each connection is enriched with calculated fields: relationship strength, dormancy status, contact categories (recruiter, executive, PE/VC).
  • •
    Pattern Matching for Categorization
    Regex patterns identify recruiters, C-suite executives, and senior leaders based on job titles and company names.
  • •
    Message History Analysis
    Cross-references your messages.csv to calculate relationship depth (strong/warm/cold) and identify dormant connections (12+ months since contact).
  • •
    Chart.js Visualizations
    Interactive charts render network composition, connection timelines, relationship distribution, and skill endorsements.
  • •
    Persistent Local Storage
    IndexedDB stores your processed data in your browser between sessions. Return anytime without re-uploading—your network analysis is instantly available.

Privacy Guarantee: LiVE contains no analytics, tracking, or external data transmission. View the source code anytime—it's all right there in the HTML file.
